About Lifecycle Systems

Enterprise expertise made accessible to independent firms

Why I Started Lifecycle Systems

After spending years solving operational problems at JPMorgan and Evelyn Partners—organizations managing billions in assets—I saw a recurring pattern: the most expensive, time-consuming challenges weren't technology problems. They were workflow problems.

At Evelyn Partners, one of the UK's largest integrated wealth management and professional services groups, I led automation initiatives that transformed how we handled everything from major incidents to client onboarding. The results were dramatic—operations that previously took weeks now happened in days, and teams that were drowning in administrative work could finally focus on clients.

But here's what struck me: the smaller wealth management firms I spoke with were facing the exact same problems—disjointed systems, manual handoffs, bottlenecks in the client lifecycle—but they didn't have access to the same caliber of solutions. They'd get quoted £100,000+ by the big consultancies, who'd then take 12-18 months to deliver something that still didn't quite fit.

That's the gap Lifecycle Systems fills. I bring the same institutional-grade automation and AI tools I deployed at £2.5bn+ firms, but tailored for independent wealth managers at a fraction of the cost and timeline. You get enterprise capability without enterprise complexity or enterprise pricing.

Independent firms shouldn't have to choose between exceptional client service and operational efficiency. With the right systems, you can have both.
